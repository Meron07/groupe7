About us

Inova is a newly formed company focused around frugal innovation. In order to spread our experience with frugal innovation, we host innovation camps all around the Nordic countries.

At Inova we value universal design and accessibility. Our innovation camps help us discover new talent and fresh ideas. It is also a good place for networking and getting to know the industry.

As early leaders of human-centered design, we keep people at the center of our work. It's a key tenet of design thinking, and even as our methods evolve in response to new, complex challenges, we’re always designing solutions for people first. We’re building to learn, and learning as we build, through inspiration, ideation, and implementation.
